Local Moves

Local moves are normally considered moves that are no greater than 50 miles in distance. These moves are calculated by the hour. We have a three hour minimum on all moves unless a flat rate is given. The hourly rate starts when we reach your door and continues, apart from breaks, until the completion of services, including the time spent driving between the loading site and the destination.

Long Distance Moves

Moves of over 50 miles are charged on a weight basis, and require a physical estimate to quote a price. We can give you some general idea of cost based on a phone survey, but keep in mind that in no case will such estimates be guaranteed without an on-site survey being performed.

Labor Only

If you need to load or unload your own truck, pod or container,we will be more than happy to help you.The hourly rates for these services do not start until we arrive at your door and there is a three-hour minimum.

Packing/Unpacking Jobs

In addition to our other services, we offer home and/or business packing/unpacking services. There is a 3 hour minimum for this service and a surcharge for bringing out packing materials. Furthermore, you can purchase all of your packing materials directly from us.
